Introduction to the Project

For my Final Project, I am going to create a mood lamp. I have made a sketch on how I want my mood lamp to be. As the days progress, I will post my updates and my progress on how I am making this mood lamp of mine. The mood lamp will function such that whenever I am near, the color of the lamp will turn blue and the LCD will appear "Welcome Aidi". The color blue represent comfort and welcoming. However, when I am away, the mood lamp will changed to color red and shows "Goodbye Aidi". This shows like the mood lamp is angry or sad whenever I am about to leave. This is my initial idea of using different types of LED lights, measuring distance of a person near the lamp and potraying messages on the LCD. As the project progress I might add a few more changes or upgrade to the lamp.
