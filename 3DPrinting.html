3D Printing

For 3D printing, I have learned to use Ultimate Cura. It is an app that allow us to print our saved design which I used from Fusion 360 to be printed out. The file format we use is STL. The printer we use is the Ultimaker 2+.

My group decided to print some of our design items. I have chosen the items on top. One of if is a keychain with my name on it. The item is small and can be convinient to bring around. Another item that i have chosen is the sphere in a box. The sphere could not be taken out.
